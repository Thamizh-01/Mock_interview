        Interview Tips

        Research the Company

        Study the company's mission, values, recent news, and culture before your interview.

        Use the STAR Method

        Structure behavioral answers with Situation, Task, Action, and Result.

        Practice Out Loud

        Rehearse your answers verbally to build confidence and refine your delivery.

        Prepare Questions

        Have thoughtful questions ready for the interviewer about the role and team.

        Dress Professionally

        Choose appropriate attire that matches the company culture.

        Follow Up

        Send a thank-you email within 24 hours of your interview.
